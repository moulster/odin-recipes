How to make some good ass coffee

I bet you're addicted, that's okay, we all kinda are bro, the secret to get as much caffeine out of your coffee as possible is to let it sit before drinking it, let the beans release that good good.

You should get harder roast if you like the bitter taste, otherwise get the lighter roast, both have around the same caffeine so it'll be okay w/e you get.

Ingredients

  • Coffee of your choice
  • Filtered water
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Firstly, boil the water, after it boils let it sit for a minute so it comes down to temperature
  2. Now comes the brewing, pour 200 ml of water over 15 grams of coffee per cup, and let it sit for 4 minutes, then stir it and let it sit for another 5 minutes
  3. There you go, now that's some well extracted shit, make sure to drink it as it comes down in temp, it'll taste better. If it tastes too bitter, add a very very small pinch of salt, it'll make it sweeter.
